What is fried rice?

Image Description:

This is fried rice with Indonesian style, we have a lot extra stuff like sausage, kerupuk, cucumber, chilli, and much other ingredients.

List of ingredients: (You can adjust according to your need)

  • 2 plates of white rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 50 gr diced chicken fillet
  • 6 peeled shrimp
  • 2 sausage spikes cut into pieces
  • 2 scallion stalks roughly cut
  • 1 tbsp margarine + 2 tbsp oil for sautéing

    • Spices sliced and grated:

  • 2 grated garlic cloves
  • 3 grated shallots
  • 1 large red chili sliced round
  • 2 cayenne pepper sliced round

    • Seasoning sauce, soy sauce and powder:

  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp stock powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground pepper
  • 1/2 tsp granulated sugar (option)

Steps:

  1. Melt margarine sauté chicken and shrimp pieces until they change color, add sausage, stir well until cooked, set aside.

  2. Heat the oil to make scrambled eggs set aside to the edge of the pan, add shallots and garlic sauté until fragrant, add sliced chilies and spring onions stir well.

  3. Add shrimp sausage chicken stir well add rice give all seasoning duo sauce soy sauce pepper powder broth powder stir quickly until smooth check the taste serve.

  4. Serve warm with side dishes.
